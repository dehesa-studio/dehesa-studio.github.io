Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Dehesa Studio (Andrea Demaria, Gran Canaria, Spain) builds tools that respect your privacy. This policy applies to all apps and services we provide, including TubeSum and Reforesta.

1. Information we collect

TubeSum

  • YouTube URLs – submitted by you to generate summaries. We do not store videos or transcripts after processing.
  • Usage data – anonymised analytics (e.g., number of requests, error rates) to improve the service.

Reforesta

  • Account data – email address, username, and hashed password (we never store plain‑text passwords).
  • Planting records – species, quantity, date, location name, optional GPS coordinates, photos (if uploaded), and survival status.
  • Seed & propagation diary – entries you create (notes, soil type, etc.).
  • Images uploaded for identification – sent to the PlantNet API; we do not store them permanently unless you explicitly save them as part of a planting record.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services (e.g., showing your plantings on a map, identifying species).
  • To authenticate you and keep your data secure.
  • To communicate important service updates (rare).
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Third‑party services

  • PlantNet API – images you upload for identification are sent to PlantNet’s servers. Their privacy policy applies: plantnet.org/privacy.
  • Railway – our hosting provider. Your data resides on Railway’s infrastructure in the EU. Railway Privacy Policy.
  • PostgreSQL – managed database; all data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

4. Data retention

  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting ops@demaria.link. We will remove your personal information within 30 days.
  • Planting records that are made public on the community map may be anonymised (username removed) but remain visible if you choose to keep them public.

5. Your rights (GDPR / LOPD)

If you are in the European Union or Spain, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten).
  • Object to processing or request restriction.
  • Data portability.

To exercise these rights, email ops@demaria.link with “Privacy request” in the subject line.

6. Children’s privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at https://dehesa.studio/privacy. Significant changes will be notified via email or a notice on the apps.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Applies to TubeSum and all Dehesa Studio products

The short version: We collect only your email address and a hashed password. We never sell your data. We never store the YouTube videos you summarise. You can delete your account and all your data at any time, instantly.

1. Who we are

Dehesa Studio is an independent software studio based in Gran Canaria, Spain, operated by Andrea Demaria. We build AI-powered tools including TubeSum (tubesum.dehesa.dev).

Contact: ops@demaria.link
Website: dehesa.dev

2. What data we collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide the service:

  • Email address — used to identify your account
  • Password — stored as a one-way hash (we cannot read your password)
  • Usage count — number of summaries used per day (not linked to content)
  • Subscription tier — free or Pro

We do NOT store:

  • The YouTube URLs you summarise
  • The transcripts of videos
  • The summaries generated
  • Your browsing history or behaviour
  • Your IP address
  • Any payment card details (handled entirely by Lemon Squeezy)

Your summaries and history are stored only in your browser's localStorage on your own device. We never see them.

3. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

We process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance (Article 6(1)(b)) — your email and password are necessary to provide the account-based service you signed up for
  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)) — usage counts are necessary to enforce the freemium limits fairly

4. How we use your data

  • To authenticate you when you log in
  • To track your daily usage against the free tier limit (3/day)
  • To manage your Pro subscription if you upgrade
  • We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or advertising

5. Third-party processors

We use the following services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Railway (railway.app) — backend hosting, located in the United States. Your email and hashed password are stored on Railway's infrastructure. Railway Privacy Policy →
  • Lemon Squeezy — payment processing. As Merchant of Record, they handle all payment data independently. We never receive your card details. Lemon Squeezy Privacy Policy →
  • Vercel — frontend hosting. Serves the web interface. Vercel Privacy Policy →
  • Webshare — proxy service used to fetch YouTube transcripts. Does not receive or process your personal data.

We have no advertising partners, analytics providers, or data brokers. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any tracking scripts.

6. Data retention

  • Active accounts — retained while your account exists
  • Session tokens — expire automatically after 30 days
  • Usage records — retained for 90 days then automatically deleted
  • Deleted accounts — email anonymised immediately, all records wiped

7. Your rights under GDPR

As a resident of the EU/EEA, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — delete your account and all associated data
  • Right to restrict processing — limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, email us at ops@demaria.link. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Authority (AEPD): aepd.es

8. Delete your account

You can delete your account at any time from within the TubeSum app. Go to Account settings → Delete account. This will immediately and permanently:

  • Anonymise your email address
  • Wipe your password hash
  • Delete all usage records
  • Invalidate all active sessions

Alternatively, email ops@demaria.link and we will delete your account manually within 48 hours.

9. Cookies

TubeSum does not use cookies. Authentication uses a token stored in your browser's localStorage. This is not a cookie and is not transmitted to third parties.

Your summary history is also stored in localStorage on your device only. Clearing your browser data will remove it.

10. Children

TubeSum is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has registered, contact us at ops@demaria.link.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Significant changes will be communicated by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.

Reforesta

A web app for guerrilla reforesters in the Canary Islands. Browse 3,500+ native species extracted from BIOCAN, CENTINELA, & EXOS databases and identify plants via PlantNet, log plantings with GPS/photos, and see community pins on a live map. Built for real‑world field work – not just dashboards.

The person behind it

Rooted in
operations,
growing into AI

I'm Andrea de la Dehesa Demaria — operations strategist, AI builder, and resident of Gran Canaria. I spent 12+ years making complex systems run smoothly, from ISO 27001 certifications to 90,000-person events.

Now I'm finishing an MSc in AI Business & Innovation and building tools that apply that same problem-solving instinct to software — things that are useful, honest, and don't waste your time.

The name Dehesa is mine by heritage — de la Dehesa is the piece of my full Spanish name that bureaucracies kept erasing. A dehesa is also a managed landscape where nothing is wasted and everything connects. That felt right for a studio.

I'm open to AI and operations roles in Europe. If what you're building needs someone who understands both the systems and the humans inside them, let's talk.
